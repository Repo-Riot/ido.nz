FAQs

Because planning a wedding comes with so many questions — and we've got answers.

How do we get started?

Easy peasy — just reach out! We'll set up an initial consultation where we chat about your vision, answer any questions, and walk you through how the planning process works. No pressure, no overwhelm — just good vibes and solid guidance from the start.

What services do you offer?

From full-service planning to on-the-day coordination, we offer a range of packages to suit your needs — whether you're hands-off, hands-on, or somewhere in-between. Explore our Services here.

Do you plan weddings in specific locations only?

Nope! While we're based in New Zealand, we love a good road trip (or flight) and happily plan weddings across the country — and beyond. Destination dreams? Let's make them happen.

What do your services cost?

It depends on the level of support you need and the scale of your day. Our Services page has a general overview, but we'll happily provide a custom quote once we've had a proper chat. No surprises, no stress. Contact us for a tailored quote.

Can I hire you for just part of the planning?

Absolutely! We know every couple's situation is different, and we're all about flexibility. If you've already locked in a few things and just need help with the rest, we'll create a package that fits.

How far in advance should I book?

As soon as you're ready! Popular dates fill fast (especially peak season), so we recommend booking early to secure your spot. A signed contract and deposit are required to confirm your booking.

Can you help with destination weddings?

Yes, yes, yes. Whether you're saying "I Do" barefoot on the beach or in a Tuscan vineyard, we'll handle all the details — so you can enjoy the experience instead of worrying about the logistics.

How involved will we be in the planning process?

That's totally up to you. Want to be in the loop every step of the way? Great. Prefer to hand it all over and show up looking fabulous? Also great. We adapt to your style and pace.

Still have questions?

Contact us here and we'll get back to you as soon as we can. We'd love to chat!
