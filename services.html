Services & Packages

Whatever stage of the planning journey you're at, we've got a package to match.

Full Planning & Day-Of Coordination

For couples who want expert support from start to finish — and then some.

This is the all-in, all-over-it package. You'll have me with you from the early planning stages right through to the last dance (and pack down).

We'll start by locking in your dream venue and vendors, creating a planning checklist and timeline, and working through all the logistics together. I'll guide you through every big decision, every tiny detail, and help you bring your unique vision to life — without the overwhelm.

Come wedding day, I'll be there to manage setup, coordinate your vendors, run your timeline, and make sure everything is exactly how you imagined it. You just enjoy the moment.

What's included:

  • Venue search + walkthrough(s) to discuss your vision
  • Vendor sourcing, booking & full management
  • Full planning checklist + timeline creation
  • Logistics and detail management throughout
  • On-site ceremony rehearsal coordination
  • Full setup and pack down
  • Wedding day timeline execution & vendor coordination
  • Complete on-the-day management

Your job? Enjoy the process. I'll keep everything on track (and beautiful).

Creative Planning with Styling Support

For the couple who likes to be involved, but not overwhelmed.

So... you're not into planning every detail? Great news — we are.

This option is perfect if you'd rather hand over the logistics and focus on the fun parts (hello, mood boards and tastings). We'll help you find and secure your venues, create your event layout, and design a style that reflects you, not just the latest trends.

You'll still be involved in the big-picture decisions — we'll just make it all easier (and prettier).

Elopements & Intimate Gatherings

Small guest list. Big heart.

Fewer people, deeper meaning, just as much magic.

If you're planning a small, heartfelt wedding or elopement with your nearest and dearest, we're still all in. This package includes full planning and styling, tailored to more intimate moments — venue, florals, styling, and all those thoughtful details that make it feel like you two.

Less stress. More meaning. All love.

Wedding Day Coordination

For couples who've planned it all and want a pro to pull it together.

You've done the planning — now it's time to hand things over and actually enjoy your day.

I'll step in a few weeks before your wedding to help tie it all together: making sure your vendors are all on the same page, attending your rehearsal, and managing everything on the day so you don't have to answer a single logistics question (seriously, none).

What's included:

  • A dedicated wedding coordinator (me!) by your side
  • Venue visit and run-through to align on your vision
  • Vendor coordination and check-ins 2–3 weeks out
  • On-site ceremony rehearsal coordination
  • On-the-day setup oversight (pack down can be arranged too)
  • 8 hours of wedding day coordination (extra hours available)
  • Main point of contact for the day — so you don't have to be
  • Ensuring everything is in place before your guests arrive
  • Handling any hiccups before you even know they happened

You focus on the love. I'll handle the logistics. Sound good?
